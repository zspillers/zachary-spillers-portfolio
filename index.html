About

Spaces worth
returning to.

Zachary Spillers is an architectural and interior designer with over twenty years of experience shaping the spaces where people live, gather, and eat — from custom residences and multi-family developments to restaurants and commercial interiors. Rooted in a family practice that began with hospitality design, his work is driven by a conviction that well-designed spaces are quietly transformative — felt before they're understood.

This portfolio spans 2008–2025 and reflects a career built across markets, scales, and typologies, always in pursuit of environments worth returning to.
